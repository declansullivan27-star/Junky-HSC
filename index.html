Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Everything people ask before they book Kansas City junk removal with us.

How much does junk removal cost in Kansas City?
Pricing is based on how much space your junk takes up in our trailer — starting at $100 for a few items, up to $450 for a full trailer, plus small add-ons for things like appliances or stairs. You get a starting price online in about 30 seconds. All online quotes are estimates; final pricing is confirmed on-site before any work begins.
Do you offer same-day junk removal?
Yes. Same-day and next-day pickups are available across the Kansas City metro depending on crew availability. Book online or call (816) 384-5010 and we'll get you the earliest window we have.
What areas do you serve?
The whole Kansas City metro on both sides of the state line — Kansas City MO, Kansas City KS, Overland Park, Olathe, Shawnee, Lenexa, Leawood, Prairie Village, Lee's Summit, Independence, Blue Springs, Liberty, Grandview, Belton and Raymore.
What kind of junk do you haul away?
Furniture, mattresses, appliances, yard waste, construction and remodeling debris, garage and estate cleanouts, hot tubs, tires and more. If it's legal to haul, we'll take it — and we donate and recycle whatever we can.
Do I have to pay a deposit?
No deposit. You book for free and pay in person — cash or Venmo — when the job is done, only after you've seen the work finished and approved the final price.
Do I need to move the junk to the curb?
Nope. Our crew does all the lifting and loading — from the basement, attic, backyard or wherever it sits. Just point, and consider it gone.
Do I need to be home for the pickup?
No — you don't need to be there. Just make sure the items are accessible when we arrive and we'll handle everything. We'll text you when the load is gone, and you can pay via Venmo whenever it's convenient for you.
